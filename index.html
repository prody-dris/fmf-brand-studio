How mSaathi speaks

To developers

FMF gives your Flutter app offline-first access to Frappe. Install Control on your Frappe server (v14+), add the SDK, ship. Control is AGPL-3.0; the SDK is MIT, so your client code stays yours.

To field users

Works offline. Your entries save to the device and sync automatically when signal returns. If a sync fails, the app tells you and keeps your data safe.
